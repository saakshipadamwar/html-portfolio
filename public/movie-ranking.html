The Best Movies according to Saakshi

My top 3 movies of all-time

Drishyam

A mystery and thriller movie which revolves around a normal family who is accused of murder.

De Dana Dan

Comedy movie with a lot of famous bollywood stars. Creates humour based on a lot of confusion among the characters.

Om Shanti Om

Masala movie, typical Bollywood movie which showcases the story of rebirth of the lead roles, involves mystery.
